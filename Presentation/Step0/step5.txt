The events are received by the subscribers

* The Created event handler will delete the invoice
* Reqest path:
	CreatedEventHandler::Handle(invoiceCompleted){
		CreatedRepository::Delete(invoiceId)
	}
	
* The Completed event handler will create a new invoice
* Reqest path:
	CompletedEventHandler::Handle(invoiceCompleted){
		CompletedRepository::Save(newInvoiceCompleted)
	}
